PHOTOGRAPHY

My passion for photography began when my grandmother gave me a disposable camera at our trip to the North Shore of Oahu, HI in 2006. As a 7-year-old, I was fascinated by the ability to capture a moment at the click of a button, and, after seeing them developed, the idea that I could preserve a memory with a photograph. On the first night Hannukah of 2013, I unwrapped a brand new Canon EOS Rebel T4-- my very own professional camera. Ecstatic, I spent the entire day running up and down my neighborhood and the forest in my backyard, ending up with 500 photos of trees and houses by bedtime. In high school, I began my own photography business taking portrait photos of families and student in the greater Oakland area. Starting with my personal network, I was able to expand my business to five large high schools in the area and several other outside clients.
