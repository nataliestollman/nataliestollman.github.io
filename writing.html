Opening
The bottle opens with a gunshot and fires oak at no target
in particular. Cue maestro. The woman in azure matches
the bluebells growing outside. Her chin tips towards the chandelier;
her flute touches her lips. Syrinx calls
before her in aching for her swan song. Since when has Debussy
been so persistent in pursuing her? The champagne scarcely reaches
her lips reach the flute, chin tipped, the champagne scarcely reaches—
the chandelier burst into flames. The chandelier disappears
in the flames. The chandelier holds the flame. The chandelier rains embers;
it has not rained in years. Her chin tipped towards the chandelier; an ember
settles on her fourth finger. She does not flinch.
Syrinx proceeds; she swallows.

2. sound

She lacks simple precisions like the subtle change of a current or the ability to run from the shadow that begins to chase her. She sits in a seat at the opera and claps politely at the Mozart Symphony No. 9 pleasantly surprised by the new first violinist’s cadenza oh how lucky… Are we to have such concise of a staccato and slurred of a legato and how each chord strikes the balance of a busy mind and a simple life. Cue maestro. Chords in progression and time in regression, keeping time in line with rhyme and rhythm.
A full row of empty seats decorates the balcony.
Shadows of sound ricochet and the chandelier bursts into flames. Embers torrent the symphony; it has not rained in years. Her chin tipped towards the chandelier, an ember settles on her fourth finger. She does not flinch.
